## 🎨 Design Patterns

### **Layout Patterns:**
1. **Hero Pattern**: Left content + Right image
2. **Alternating Pattern**: Content switches sides
3. **Asymmetric Pattern**: 47/53 split (Roles section)
4. **Centered Pattern**: Full-width centered content
5. **Grid Pattern**: Multiple columns with cards

### **Color Patterns:**
- **White Sections**: Hero, Insights, Data into Action, IWS, Solutions, Blog
- **Black Sections**: Why MOM, Our Process, Partner CTA, Roles, Why Choose Quartic
- **Mixed Sections**: Testimonials, Footer CTA

### **Content Patterns:**
- **Tag + Heading + Description**: Most sections
- **Interactive Elements**: Sliders, tabs, animations
- **Visual Elements**: Images, charts, diagrams
- **CTA Elements**: Buttons, links, forms

This visual structure shows the complete layout and flow of the Quartic.ai website with proper section alignment and responsive design patterns.
